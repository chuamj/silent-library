Topic: Life Is A Game, Play it. Life Is A Challenge, Meet It.

As William Shakespeare wrote, "All the world's a stage," and we, its players, navigate a rollercoaster of emotions. To master this Game of Life, we must face challenges with resilience, learn from setbacks, and continuously strive for growth, rather than being enslaved by life's difficulties.
Speaker: Ms. Rosemarie Chandran

This is a physical meet-up session.
*Note: This Eventbrite standard event listing capacity is capped at 25 pax. If the event is showing sold-out status or you have signed up under waitlist, please walk in directly to join us. We appreciate your kind understanding.
This programme is supported by the Silent Library.

SMS Knowledge Café is a place where seniors meet to have fun and productive conversations, learning from each other, and making better sense of living in an urban community. The objective of the SMS Knowledge Café is to encourage peer-led discussions and small-group learning for seniors.
